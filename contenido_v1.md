## 6. Product Philosophy

### Experimentation

Products should be built from hypotheses rather than opinions.

I design experiments, run tests and learn quickly in order to improve the user experience and product performance.

### Behavioral Design

People do not always make rational decisions.

Understanding cognitive biases and real motivations allows us to design more effective products.

### Gamification

Well-designed systems can incentivize positive behaviors.

Game dynamics can increase engagement, motivation and adoption when used correctly.

### Systems Thinking

Products are not isolated screens.

They are systems where small design decisions can generate large behavioral effects.
